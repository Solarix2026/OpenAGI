# Data Analyst Skill

## Purpose
Analyze data, generate insights, create visualizations, and provide statistical analysis. Transform raw data into actionable intelligence.

## Invocation Contract
Given a dataset or data description, this skill:
1. Explores and understands the data structure
2. Performs statistical analysis and identifies patterns
3. Creates meaningful visualizations
4. Generates actionable insights and recommendations
5. Documents methodology and findings

## Output Format
Returns comprehensive analysis with:
- Data overview and summary statistics
- Key findings and patterns identified
- Visualizations (charts, graphs, plots)
- Statistical tests and significance
- Actionable recommendations
- Methodology documentation

## Examples
**Input:** "Analyze this sales data and identify trends, seasonal patterns, and growth opportunities"

**Output:**
```
# Sales Data Analysis Report

## Data Overview
- Period: Jan 2023 - Dec 2023
- Total transactions: 15,234
- Revenue: $1,234,567
- Products: 234 unique SKUs

## Key Findings

### 1. Seasonal Patterns
- Q4 shows 40% higher sales than Q1
- Holiday season (Nov-Dec) contributes 35% of annual revenue
- Summer dip (July-August) consistent across years

### 2. Growth Trends
- Year-over-year growth: 18.5%
- Monthly average growth: 1.5%
- Accelerating trend in Q4

### 3. Product Performance
- Top 10 products: 60% of revenue
- Emerging category: Eco-friendly products (+45% YoY)
- Declining category: Legacy hardware (-12% YoY)

## Recommendations
1. Increase inventory before Q4 holiday season
2. Focus marketing on eco-friendly product line
3. Develop summer promotion strategy to address seasonal dip
4. Consider bundling top performers with emerging products

## Visualizations
[Charts would be generated here showing trends, patterns, etc.]
```
